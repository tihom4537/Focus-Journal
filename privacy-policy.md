# Privacy Policy

**App:** Focus Journal  
**Last updated:** April 25, 2026

## Overview

Focus Journal is a personal mood tracking app. Your privacy is important to us, so we kept things simple: **all your data stays on your device.**

## What We Collect

We collect nothing. Focus Journal does not:

- Collect personal information
- Connect to the internet or any server
- Use analytics or tracking
- Share data with third parties

## How Your Data Is Stored

All mood entries, tags, and journal notes are stored locally on your device using your operating system's built-in storage. No account is required.

**Deleting the app removes all data permanently.**

## Third-Party Services

None. Focus Journal has no third-party SDKs, ads, or analytics frameworks.

## Children's Privacy

Focus Journal does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this document.
